Chapter Xxviii.

-There Is A Third Kind, Consisting Of Those Animals Which Are

called uri. These are a little below the elephant in size, and of the appearance, colour, and shape of a bull. Their strength and speed are extraordinary; they spare neither man nor wild beast which they have espied. These the Germans take with much pains in pits and kill them.
